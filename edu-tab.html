Education Tab

We are introducing pre-loaded educational tab for higher studies for students. Built to help students transform the way they learn, the VinRa EduTab, enables quick assimilation and amplifies productivity by offering a wide-range of features to streamline the way students plan their study time.

The interactive, high-definition graphics support also ensures that learning never ever become a chore! Offering options to bookmark and keep track of courses covered and tests taken, this advanced education tablet makes sure that student always keep planning ahead!


Features:

  • Customised for particular university or institute
  • Personalized learning
  • Teacher to Students connectivity through tablets
  • More cost-effective than textbooks
  • Education on their fingertips and do not have to carry their books all time

EduTab Specifications:

  • version: Android V6.1.1
  • 10" IPS Display, 1280 x 800 pixels
  • RAM: 8GB/16GB
  • Expandable: Card Support Up to 32GB
  • Support: Built-in Wi-Fi, External 3G Dongle and Ethernet time
  • Support: MPEG4/MP3/WAV/AVI/MP3/TXT/PDF/JPEG/ GIF/ PNG
  • Battery: Built-in Li-Polymer battery
  • Backup: 6000 mAh, 6 Hrs

EduTab Specifications:

  • • BTech • MBA • BBA • MSc • BSc • MCom • BCom
  • • MCA • BCA • Education (B.Ed, M.Ed.) • Skill Development
